Essential Brewing Tips

Water Quality

Use filtered water heated to 195-205¬∞F for optimal extraction. Water quality affects 98% of your cup.

Coffee-to-Water Ratio

Start with 1:15 to 1:17 ratio (coffee to water) and adjust to taste preference.

Grind Consistency

Invest in a burr grinder for consistent particle size. Fresh grinding before brewing makes a huge difference.

Timing Matters

Follow recommended brew times for each method. Over-extraction leads to bitterness, under-extraction to sourness.
